Kadai Paneer is a popular, vibrant North Indian dish made with soft paneer (cottage cheese) and bell peppers

cooked in a rich, tangy tomato gravy. The dish is renowned for its bold, aromatic flavor, driven by a freshly ground spice blend called Kadai Masala

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Onion
  • Paneer Masala
